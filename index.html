About Us

Welcome to Canberra Paediatric Dentistry. We were established in 1992 and have been providing Specialist Paediatric Dental services in the ACT and surrounding NSW regions for almost 30 years. Our patients travel from the ACT and all-over regional NSW

We are specialist dental practitioners dedicated to the oral health of children, teenagers, and those with special needs.

Paediatric dentists are specialist practitioners with advanced training. Training is hospital based and completed in conjunction with an advanced university degree. Paediatric Dentistry is a National Dental Board recognized specialty of dentistry.
