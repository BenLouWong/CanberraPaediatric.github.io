What to do at home

It is important that parents educate their children about teeth and oral health care. Good habits will follow your child for the rest of their life! Some things you can do at home are:

Brush their teeth twice a day

Brush all their teeth, not just the front ones

Floss their teeth at least once a day

Cut back on sugar, snacks and soft drinks

Drink plenty of water every day
