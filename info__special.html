Children with Special Needs

Here at Canberra Paediatric Dentistry, we aim to create a warm, therapeutic environment for your child. We provide comprehensive care to children with special needs, including:

  • Medical conditions such as Autism, Down Syndrom, Cerebral Palsy and ADHD
  • Physical or behavioural difficulties and disabilities
  • Oro-facial anomalies or syndromes
